5. Dika is...than the cleaning service man. a.hord workingb.more hard workingc.most hard workingd.the most hard working​


• Dika is more hard working than the cleaning service man.

Pembahasan

• Digunakan "more hard working" karena pada soal tersebut membangdingkan (compare) Dika dengan cleaning service man, yang artinya soal ini berbentuk comparative. Comparative adalah bentuk grammar yang digunakan untuk membandingkan 2 orang, binatang, benda, dll. Comparative sentence umumnya menggunakan kata berakhiran -er dan more.

Example

1. She is smarter than me.

2. Two is better than one.

3. She is more diligent than me.

6. Pengertian working model pengertian working model


Working model adalah representasi fisik atau visual yang digunakan untuk menggambarkan dan menjelaskan cara kerja atau prinsip dasar suatu sistem, produk, atau konsep. Working model biasanya dirancang dan dibuat agar dapat menunjukkan secara konkret bagaimana suatu objek atau sistem bekerja.

Working model dapat berupa prototipe, sketsa, maquette, atau model yang dibuat dengan menggunakan bahan-bahan seperti kayu, plastik, atau bahan lainnya. Tujuan utama dari working model adalah untuk memberikan gambaran yang lebih nyata dan visual tentang cara kerja suatu sistem atau produk, sehingga memudahkan pemahaman dan penjelasan kepada orang lain.

Working model juga digunakan dalam proses pengembangan dan perancangan produk. Dengan membuat working model, desainer dan insinyur dapat menguji dan mengoreksi desain sebelum produk akhir diproduksi dalam skala besar.

Overall, working model merupakan alat yang efektif untuk memvisualisasikan dan memahami prinsip kerja suatu sistem atau produk dengan cara yang lebih praktis dan konkret.

27. What would happen to your body if your not properposture an overwork our bones or muscles?​


1. Back, Neck, and Shoulder Pain. Poor posture or slouching can lead to tension and pain in your neck, upper back, lower back, and shoulders. Be careful that you don't overcorrect on your own wrongly by pulling your shoulders back too far as this could cause muscle tension and stiffness.

2. POOR CIRCULATION

If you sit for hours, especially if it’s daily, in a chair with poor posture, you’re putting yourself at risk of developing or worsening circulation problems. By sitting with poor posture all day, you’re keeping your body from obtaining the necessary circulation it requires.  

Poor posture can also make you vulnerable to varicose veins.  

3. IMPAIRED LUNG FUNCTION

Poor posture can affect the amount of air you’re taking into your lungs when breathing. Leaning or hunching forward all the time can negatively influence your lung function and capacity. When your lungs aren’t functioning as they should be, your brain, heart and other vital organs won’t get the oxygen they need.

This could lead to shortness of breath, poor cognitive function and even heart and vascular disease.

4. POOR DIGESTION

If you have a desk job then you likely know how easy it is to slouch your shoulders and neck over your abdomen and chest. When you slouch, you compress your abdominal organs, including your digestive tract.

Doing this can over time, negatively impact your metabolism and your ability to process foods properly, potentially resulting in a nutritional deficiency.

5. CONSTRICTED NERVES

The long-term impact of poor posture can cause your spine and other bones to shift their position. This is because your skeletal system starts coming into contact with your surrounding nerves, “pinching” them.

The “pinched” nerves can not only lead to back and neck pain, but could also cause pain in other parts of your body.

6. MISALIGNED SPINE

Bad posture can negatively impact your spine’s position. When you have proper alignment, you can easily feel posture throughout your musculoskeletal system. But, when if you perform most of your everyday activities with bad body mechanics or are stressed or injured in any way, you could end up with spinal misalignment and muscle spasm.

37. Abdillah....in London but now he is working at his house in Jakarta A.work B.are working C.works D.Is working


Abdillah works in London bot now he is working at his house in Jakarta

Penjelasan:

Untuk penjelasan dari penggalan kalimat di atas, terdapat 2 jenis tense. Yaitu Simple Present Tense dan Present Continuous Tense.

Perbedaan dari kedua tense ini adalah Simple Present Tense cenderung menjelaskan kejadian berulang-ulang pada satu waktu dan merupakan aktivitas yang selalu dilakukan. Sedangkan, Present Continuous Tense cenderung menjelaskan suatu kejadian yang sedang terjadi pada masa sekarang.

Rumus dari Simple Present Tense adalah :

(+) = S + V1/V(s)/V(es)(-) = S + don't/doesn't + V1(?) = Do/Does + S + V1

Rumus dari Present Continuous Tense adalah :

(+) = S + to be + V(ing)(-) = S + to be + not + V(ing)(?) = to be + S + V(ing)

• Pada kalimat pertama, menggunakan Simple Present Tense. Karena Abdillah biasa dan selalu bekerja di London.

• Pada kalimat kedua, menggunakan Present Continuous Tense. Karena, ada keterangan waktu "now".
